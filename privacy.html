Velura Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data We Collect

Velura collects menstrual cycle dates, skin log entries, and skin type information you provide. Health data derived from Apple HealthKit is stored encrypted on your device using iOS SecureStore.

Data Storage

Skin photos and health data never leave your device. Non-sensitive preferences are stored locally using AsyncStorage.

Third Parties

Velura uses RevenueCat to process subscription payments. No personal health data is shared with RevenueCat or any third party.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings. This permanently removes all local data including health data and skin logs.

Contact

For privacy questions contact: support@lumislabs.in
